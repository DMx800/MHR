Network Rail Network Rail owns and operates Britain’s rail infrastructure. Rail Delivery Group The Rail Delivery Group (RDG) is an industry association that advocates on behalf of its member companies and Network Rail. It provides the Train Companies with impartial services such as National Rail Enquiries, Rail Settlement Plan and Rail Staff Travel. The Train Drivers Academy The Train Drivers Academy is the official government scheme centred around improving the management of training and recruitment for train drivers. It is not a physical establishment but rather a cloud-based and virtual centre for train driver training. Train Operating Companies To find out about roles with specific train companies visit the Rail Delivery Group page about train company careers. There you will find a list of job vacancies, as well as links to the careers pages of each train company’s website.
